About Hibelive Carton Packaging

Founded: 2022

Founder: A hands-on founder passionate about creative packaging solutions.

Where we serve: Lagos and nationwide on request

Hibelive started after graduation and NYSC with a simple idea: provide tailored, durable, and visually appealing carton packaging for Nigerian businesses — from food vendors to retailers and manufacturers.

Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and high-quality carton packaging solutions tailored to customer needs.

Vision

To become a leading carton packaging brand in Nigeria known for innovation, reliability, and customer satisfaction.

How we work

  1. Tell us your needs: Provide carton size, quantity, intended use, and any printing ideas.
  2. Digital sample: We create a digital proof for your approval for printed/custom orders.
  3. Production: Once approved and deposit is received, production begins (3–7 working days typical).
  4. Delivery: We deliver within Lagos and can arrange nationwide delivery on request.

Design & Finishes

We support logo placement and basic design guidance. Available finishes: lamination (matte/gloss), die-cutting, and UV coating for larger runs.

Minimum Order

MOQ varies by carton type; custom printed orders generally start at 100 pieces.
